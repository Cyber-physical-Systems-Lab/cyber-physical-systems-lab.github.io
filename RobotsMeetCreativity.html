Art Residency Call: Robots Meet Creativity

Are you an artist or art student ready to push boundaries at the intersection of robotics and creative expression?

The Cyber-Physical Systems Lab at Uppsala University invites visionary artists to explore the creative potential of robotic systems through our open art residency program.

What We Offer

  • Access to cutting-edge robotics: Work hands-on with our diverse fleet of robotic platforms - from mobile robots to drone swarms and collaborative robotic systems
  • Technical expertise and mentorship: Collaborate directly with robotics researchers who are passionate about bridging technology and art
  • Lab resources and equipment: Full support to develop and realize demonstrators for your art projects
  • Flexible residency: 2 - 6 months tailored to your project needs
  • Interdisciplinary environment: Join a dynamic research lab where innovation meets creativity

Who Should Apply

Artists, designers, and creative practitioners who:
  • Have a compelling vision for integrating robotics into artistic practice
  • Want to explore how autonomous systems can become mediums for creative expression
  • Seek to challenge conventional boundaries between art, technology, and society
  • Are curious about the inner workings of a robotics research lab

How to Apply

This is a rolling call - applications accepted year-round.
  1. Explore our robotic capabilities: Robot Colleagues and Projects
  2. Send us your application via email including:
    • Your CV
    • A project proposal (minimum 3 pages) describing your artistic vision and how you envision using our robotic systems
